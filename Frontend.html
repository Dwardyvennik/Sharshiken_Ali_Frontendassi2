Actor and Musician



Michael C. Hall

About Michael

Michael Carlyle Hall (born February 1, 1971) is an American actor and musician. He is best known for his roles as Dexter Morgan on the Showtime series Dexter and David Fisher on the HBO series Six Feet Under.

Notable Career Events

  1. 1999 - Landed breakthrough role as David Fisher in Six Feet Under
  2. 2006 - Began starring as Dexter Morgan in Dexter
  3. 2010 - Won a Golden Globe for Best Actor for Dexter
  4. 2013 - Made Broadway debut in the musical Hedwig and the Angry Inch
  5. 2021 - Returned to play Dexter Morgan in Dexter: New Blood

Key Contributions

  • Portrayal of complex, morally ambiguous characters
  • Advocacy for cancer research and awareness
  • Work in both television and theater
  • Musical performances with band Princess Goes
